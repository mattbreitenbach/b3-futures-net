## DI1 & DAP

- Formula original
    - $P0 = \frac{100.000}{(1+\frac{i}{100})^\frac{du}{252}}$


- Formula para taxa
    - $i = (\frac{100.000}{P0})^{252/du}-1$


## DDI & DCO

- Formula original
    - $P0 = \frac{100.000}{(i*\frac{dc}{360})}+1$


- Formula para taxa
    - $i = (\frac{100.000}{P0}-1)*\frac{360}{dc}$
